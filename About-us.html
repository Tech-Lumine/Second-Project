ABOUT US

Where Creativity Meets Community

Our Mission

Global Art Coterie (GAC) is dedicated to fostering a global community of artists by providing mentorship, securing sponsorship, and building sustainable partnerships. We aim to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development, aligning with the 17th Sustainable Development Goal.O

Our Vision

To be a leading global platform that empowers creatives through robust support systems, enabling them to thrive and contribute to sustainable development worldwide.

OUR CORE VALUES

Empowerment

Providing resources and opportunities for artists to succeed.

Innovation

Encouraging creativity and innovative approaches in the arts.

Community

Building a supportive and collaborative network of creatives.

Integrity

Upholding transparency, accountability, and ethical practices.
